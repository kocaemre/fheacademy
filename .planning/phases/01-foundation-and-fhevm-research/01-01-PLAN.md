
    - Do NOT create a `tailwind.config.js` or `tailwind.config.ts` file -- Tailwind v4 uses CSS-first config
    - Do NOT use `@tailwind base; @tailwind components; @tailwind utilities;` -- use `@import "tailwindcss"`
    - Do NOT use `tailwindcss` as a PostCSS plugin -- use `@tailwindcss/postcss`
